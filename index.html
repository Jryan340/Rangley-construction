About

50 Years of Craft.
Now Rooted in Rangeley.

The Rangeley Lakes region is one of the most pristine landscapes in the Northeast — glacial lakes surrounded by spruce, birch, and granite, with views that stretch across mountain ridgelines. It takes a builder who knows his craft to do it justice.

With over fifty years of construction experience, Ryan Home Builders now calls the Rangeley Lakes region home. From timber-frame cabins perched above the waterline to four-season retreats built to handle heavy snow loads, we bring a lifetime of expertise to every build on these shores.
