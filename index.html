The Last Great
Lakefront in New England

Rangeley Lake sits at 1,500 feet in the western mountains of Maine — 6,000 acres of pristine water surrounded by spruce, birch, and granite. Saddleback Mountain rises just minutes away. Fall foliage, dark skies, loon calls at dawn. This is the Maine people dream about.

With fifty years of building experience, Ryan Home Builders chose this place to call home. We're building custom cabins for people who want more than a getaway — they want a legacy on the lake.
